Services of Indian Railways

Indian Railways provides a wide variety of services catering to different needs. These include:

  • Passenger Trains: From local trains to express trains, offering comfortable and affordable travel.
  • Freight Services: Handling the transportation of goods and commodities across the country.
  • Luxury Trains: Trains like the Palace on Wheels offer an exquisite, royal travel experience.
  • Special Services: Indian Railways also offers special services like tourist trains, executive trains, and more.
