Introduction to Indian Railways

Indian Railways is one of the largest railway networks in the world. It serves millions of people across India daily, providing connectivity, economic growth, and cultural exchange. With a vast network of over 65,000 kilometers, it plays a key role in the country’s transportation sector.

It’s not just a means of transport, but also an integral part of India’s heritage, offering an opportunity to experience the diversity and beauty of the country. Indian Railways continues to evolve and modernize, aiming to provide a safe, efficient, and sustainable travel experience for its passengers.
