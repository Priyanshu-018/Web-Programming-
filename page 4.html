Future Plans of Indian Railways

Indian Railways has ambitious plans for the future. Some key initiatives include:

  • Electrification: Aiming to fully electrify its routes to reduce carbon emissions.
  • High-Speed Trains: The introduction of bullet trains like the Mumbai-Ahmedabad corridor.
  • Modern Stations: Renovating and modernizing stations for better passenger experience.
  • Smart Technology: Implementing AI, big data, and IoT to enhance efficiency and services.
