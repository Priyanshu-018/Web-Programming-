History of Indian Railways

The history of Indian Railways dates back to 1853 when the first passenger train ran from Mumbai to Thane. This marked the beginning of the rail network, which played a vital role in the industrialization and modernization of India. Over the years, Indian Railways expanded, connecting the remotest corners of the country.

Since its inception, it has witnessed significant milestones, including the introduction of new trains, stations, and technological advancements. Today, Indian Railways is a national pride, continuing its journey towards providing better services and infrastructure for millions of passengers.
